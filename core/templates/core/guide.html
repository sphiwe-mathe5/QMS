Booking System User Guide

1

Account Access

Before making a booking, you'll need to access your account:

  • New Users: Click the "Register" button and complete the registration form with your details.
  • Existing Users: Use the "Login" button and enter your credentials.
Make sure to verify your email address after registration to access all booking features.
2

Booking Process

Follow these steps to make your booking:

  • Find the "Book Slots" Button: Located at the top of your dashboard.
  • Select Date: Browse through available dates in the calendar view.
  • Choose Time: Available time slots will be displayed for your selected date.
  • Confirm Booking: Click "Book Slot" to secure your reservation.
Important: After booking, you'll receive a unique reference number. This reference is crucial for collection - write it down or save it in your phone.

Your booking details page will show:

  • Booking reference number
  • Selected date and time
  • Status of your booking
  • Collection instructions
3

Managing Your Booking

Important information about your booking:

  • View your current booking in the "My Bookings" section
  • Cancel your booking through the "Cancel Booking" button if needed
  • Track the status of your booking (Active/Expired)
Please Note:
  • You cannot make a new booking until you collect or cancel your current booking
  • Bookings automatically expire if not collected on the scheduled date
  • Expired bookings must be cancelled before making a new reservation
